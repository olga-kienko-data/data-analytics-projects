#  Sales Data Visualization with Matplotlib

##  Project Overview
This project focuses on visualizing e-commerce sales data using Python and Matplotlib.

The goal is to explore different aspects of the dataset and identify patterns in sales, customer behavior, and profitability through visual analysis.

---

##  Dataset
The dataset contains information about online store transactions, including:

- order_date, time
- product_category, product
- sales, profit, quantity
- customer and device information
- shipping and payment details

---

##  Visualizations

### 1. Sales Distribution (Histogram)
- Shows the distribution of sales values
- Identifies common price ranges and skewness

---

### 2. Sales Over Time (Line Chart)
- Displays sales trends over time
- Helps identify growth or decline patterns

---

### 3. Orders by Category (Bar Chart)
- Compares number of orders across product categories
- Identifies most and least popular categories

---

### 4. Profit vs Sales (Scatter Plot)
- Shows relationship between profit and sales
- Helps understand correlation between variables

---

### 5. Combined Visualization (2x2 Grid)
- All charts displayed together
- Allows quick comparison of insights

---

##  Key Insights
- Sales distribution is right-skewed (many small purchases, few large ones)
- Sales show fluctuations over time
- Some categories dominate in order volume
- Positive relationship between profit and sales

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- Python (pandas, matplotlib)
